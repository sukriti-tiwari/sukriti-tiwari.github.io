Reflections of
an artist

I invested time in learning fabric painting, pencil shading, charcoal painting, and ceramic work on my own. It does take time and a lot of experimentation but after practice one gets the hold. Art school taught me watercolor and oil painting. To this date I prefer watercolor over other mediums. The texture and natural look appeal to me. I believe art must reflect our surroundings. Every object can be converted into something artsy. Art, to me, is an important aspect of life. Apart from formal training as an artist, I have also been conducting art and craft classes at home and calligraphy classes for kids. I have also organized baking, ice-cream making, ceramic work, fabric painting and oil painting classes for ladies and young girls. As I am passionate about cooking, I keep experimenting and innovating new dishes as well.
